The Property

A Broadmoor Charmer

Majestic two-story home on a large, beautifully landscaped lot in the coveted Broadmoor neighborhood — just a short walk to award-winning Broadmoor Elementary and minutes from The Broadmoor resort.

The main level offers a bright formal living room, a cozy den with hardwood floors and fireplace, an updated kitchen with stone counters and newer cabinets, and a formal dining space that opens to the back patio. A loft on the upper level is perfect for a home office.

The primary suite features a barnwood-accent fireplace wall and a full en-suite bath. Two additional large bedrooms share a beautifully updated hall bath. The finished basement adds a flex/library space, exercise room, 4th bedroom, ¾ bath, and a renovated laundry room.

Step outside to a massive covered patio with barnwood trim, a hot tub, and a lush, mature-treed yard — a true Colorado oasis.
